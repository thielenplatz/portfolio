About Sarah Thiele
In my undergraduate studies at Webster University, I focused on the balance between visual art and communication. I applied this education at The French School of St. Louis Language Immersion Schools and discovered a talent for sharing ideas with tiny humans: in French. I balanced teaching with designing for the district’s Development Department, enjoying years of experience before shifting my focus on graphic design full-time.

As a freelance designer, I realize the human element gives me the power to create my best work. Collaboration with clients and other artists is the highlight of my daily work. Armed with this new knowledge, I look forward to finding a role that couples customer service with creativity.

When I’m not working, I can be found baking, walking my two beautiful lab mixes, and reading and rereading epic novels.

Specialties: Visual communication, interpersonal relations, backwards planning, organization, classroom management, speaking French
